This is my Vindinium Website

vindinium is a website where you can code your own bot to be the best of the best! Your goal of the game is to have the most gold by the end of the 1200 turns. There are 4 players and they each also code their own bots. You can code your bot to steal enemy mines, kill other bots, or you can have both of them!
